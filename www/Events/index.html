About us

The Overall summary about the event is organizing the International women’s day hackthon in Madurai. This is the first time in South part of Tamil nadu more than 600 girls coming together to solve the issues of women’s in rural area. These girls use technology to develop a solution which can help women’s to solve the day to day problem in rural area. This is two day 36 hours hackathon focusing on developing mobile application for women safety and women powerment.
